Object Oriented Programming

This section of projects its to demonstrate the basic fundamentals of object-oriented programming in both C++ and Python. The Project is constructed as a mockup for companies to enter relevant personal information about an employee such as their name, contact information, address, degree, and both contact and address information for their alma mater. Data encapsulation, template and composition classes, inheritance, polymorphism, and operator overloading are all included in the project, and a UML compliant diagram of each class and its member functions and variables are included.
